My School

Delhi Private School, Sharjah (DPS Sharjah) is a renowned Indian curriculum school in the UAE. Established in 2000, it offers education from Pre-KG to Grade 12. With a focus on academic excellence and holistic development, DPS Sharjah boasts a strong reputation for its dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and diverse extracurricular activities. It is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and has been recognized with prestigious awards like the Khalifa Award for Excellence in Education.
